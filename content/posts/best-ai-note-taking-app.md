If you sit in a lot of meetings, an AI note-taker pays for itself in attention alone — you stop scrambling to write and let it capture, summarize, and pull out action items. Here are the best options.

## Quick picks

| You want... | Best app | Free tier |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Live transcription | Otter | Yes |
| Deep integrations / CRM | Fireflies | Yes |
| Notes in your workspace | Notion AI | Bundled |

## Otter — best for live transcription

Otter joins your call and transcribes in real time, then generates a summary you can search later. Strong on Zoom and Google Meet, with a generous free tier to start.

## Fireflies — best for integrations

Fireflies shines when you want notes to **flow into your other tools** — CRMs, project apps, Slack. It records, transcribes and routes the output automatically, which suits sales and ops teams.

## Notion AI — best if you live in Notion

If your team already runs on Notion, keeping meeting notes (and their AI summaries) **inside the same workspace** removes the copy-paste step entirely.

> **What actually matters when choosing:** not the feature list, but two things — *which meeting platform you use* (make sure it's supported) and *where you want the notes to end up*. Match those two and the rest is detail.

## How to choose

1. **Mostly Zoom/Meet and want live notes?** Otter.
2. **Want notes pushed into your CRM and tools?** Fireflies.
3. **Already on Notion?** Notion AI.

Start with a free tier — every option here has one — and upgrade only when you hit the monthly minute limits. Confirm current pricing and platform support on each official site.
