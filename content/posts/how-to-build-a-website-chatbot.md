An AI chatbot can answer your visitors' questions around the clock — and you can set one up without writing code. Here's the process.

## Step-by-step (no code)

1. **Choose a no-code chatbot platform** that supports training on your content.
2. **Add your content** — website pages, FAQs, docs, policies.
3. **Let it learn (RAG)** — the platform indexes your content so the bot answers from *your* information.
4. **Customize** — tone, greeting, branding, fallback behavior.
5. **Embed it** — paste the provided snippet (or use a plugin) on your site.
6. **Test** with real questions, then refine the source content.

## What makes a good website chatbot

- **Answers from your content**, not generic guesses
- **Knows its limits** — hands off to a human when stuck
- **Stays current** — update content as things change
- **On-brand** tone and clear greeting

## Where to keep a human

| Bot handles | Human handles |
| --- | --- |
| FAQs, hours, policies | Complaints, disputes |
| "Where's my order?" | Sensitive issues |
| Product basics | High-value sales |

An easy escape hatch to a human. Nothing frustrates people more than a chatbot that loops on a question it can't answer with no way out. Build the bot to **confidently handle the common 70%** and **gracefully hand off the rest** — "Let me connect you with someone." A bot that knows its limits earns trust; one that traps people in dead ends costs you customers.

## Bottom line

Build a no-code website chatbot by training a platform on your content, customizing it, and embedding the snippet. Let it handle common questions 24/7 — and always give visitors a clear path to a human. Confirm current pricing on your chosen platform's site.
