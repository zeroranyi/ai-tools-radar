## Start with one general assistant

A single assistant (ChatGPT or Claude) covers a huge share of marketing: copy, emails, social posts, briefs, ad variations, brainstorming. For many marketers, **this plus one channel tool is the whole stack.**

## Add channel tools only as needed

- **Ranking on Google?** Add Surfer SEO or Frase.
- **Producing branded content at volume?** Add Jasper or Copy.ai.
- **Heavy on social/video?** Add Canva AI and Opus Clip.

## Where to keep humans

AI produces; humans decide. Strategy, brand judgment, and fact-checking every claim stay with you — AI-generated marketing that's generic or inaccurate underperforms and erodes trust. Use AI to ship more, faster, with your voice on top.

## Bottom line

Build a lean marketing stack: one general assistant for content, plus the single channel tool you need most (SEO, brand voice, social or images), expanding only as volume justifies. Most tools have free tiers — start there. Explore everything in the [AI Tools Directory](/tools).
