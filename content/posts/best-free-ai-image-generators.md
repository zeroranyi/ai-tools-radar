"Free" AI image generation means two different things: tools you run yourself (truly unlimited, no watermark) and free tiers of hosted tools (easy, but limited). Here are the best of each.

## Quick picks

| You want... | Best free option |
| --- | --- |
| Unlimited, no watermark | Stable Diffusion / Flux |
| Generous free tier | Leonardo |
| Free + text in images | Ideogram |
| Easiest free credits | ChatGPT's generator |

## Truly free: Stable Diffusion & Flux

Open models you can run locally or through free web interfaces — **no watermark, no per-image fee, commercial-friendly licenses**. The trade-off is setup. Best when you want volume and control for $0.

## Easy free tiers: Leonardo & Ideogram

- **Leonardo** — generous daily free credits, strong for design and game assets.
- **Ideogram** — free tier plus the best **readable text in images** (posters, logos).

## Limited free credits

Many paid tools — including ChatGPT's built-in generator — give **limited free credits**, enough for casual use. For the full landscape (including paid leaders like Midjourney), see [best AI image generators](/posts/best-ai-image-generators) and [Midjourney alternatives](/posts/midjourney-alternatives).

## Watch the watermark and license

Two things catch people out on "free": **watermarks** on hosted free tiers, and **commercial-use limits**. Open models avoid both; hosted free tiers vary, so check the terms before using an image anywhere public.

## Bottom line

For unlimited, watermark-free generation, run Stable Diffusion or Flux; for easy free use, lean on Leonardo and Ideogram's free tiers or limited credits elsewhere. Check watermark and commercial terms before publishing. More options in the [AI Tools Directory](/tools).
