For marketing visuals, the Midjourney vs DALL·E choice comes down to **aesthetics vs ease**. Here's how to pick for your brand.

## The core difference

- **Midjourney** — **artistic quality** and polished, stylized images. The look that stops the scroll.
- **DALL·E (in ChatGPT)** — **ease and prompt-following**, plus the best **text-in-image** rendering. Describe and refine conversationally.

## Side-by-side for marketing

| | Midjourney | DALL·E (ChatGPT) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Artistic quality | Top-tier | Good |
| Ease of use | Moderate | Very easy |
| Prompt-following | Strong | Very strong |
| Text in images | Weak | Strong |
| Workflow | Separate app | Inside ChatGPT |
| Commercial use | Check license tier | Check terms |

## Choose Midjourney if...

- You want **standout, artistic** brand visuals
- Aesthetics are a competitive edge for you
- You'll invest time in the workflow

## Choose DALL·E if...

- You want **fast, easy** creation in ChatGPT
- You need **readable text** in graphics (posters, ads)
- You value prompt-following over pure artistry

If your graphics need *words* — sale banners, quote cards, ad creative with text — DALL·E (or Ideogram) saves you from the broken-text problem most generators have. If you're after a distinctive *visual identity*, Midjourney's aesthetic edge is worth the extra workflow. And whichever you pick, confirm the commercial-use license before putting images on paid campaigns.

## How to choose

1. **Want artistic, standout visuals?** Midjourney.
2. **Want easy creation + text in images?** DALL·E (ChatGPT).

Both have ways to try — confirm commercial terms and pricing on each official site.
