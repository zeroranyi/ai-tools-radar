If you spend your day in Gmail, Docs and Sheets, the Gemini vs ChatGPT question has a different answer than for everyone else. Integration changes the math.

## The deciding factor: where you work

- **Gemini** lives **inside Google Workspace**. It can draft in Docs, summarize Gmail threads, and analyze Sheets — with access to your actual content.
- **ChatGPT** is a **standalone** assistant. More versatile overall, but it doesn't natively touch your Google apps.

## Side-by-side for Workspace users

| | Gemini | ChatGPT |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Works inside Gmail/Docs/Sheets | Native | No |
| Access to your Google content | Yes | Manual paste |
| Raw versatility | Strong | Stronger |
| Ecosystem / extensions | Growing | Large |
| Free tier | Yes | Yes |

## When Gemini is the obvious pick

- You draft and edit in **Google Docs** all day
- You want AI to **summarize Gmail** and **analyze Sheets**
- You value **not copy-pasting** between an assistant and your docs

## When ChatGPT still wins

- Your work spans **many tools**, not just Google
- You need the **broadest features** (images, voice, ecosystem)
- You want the most capable all-round assistant regardless of platform

For a committed Google Workspace user, the integration is worth more than a small capability edge. The time saved by *not* shuttling text between apps adds up fast. But if Google is just one of many tools you use, don't pick Gemini for integration you won't lean on — ChatGPT's versatility serves you better.

## How to choose

1. **Live in Gmail/Docs/Sheets?** Gemini.
2. **Work across many tools?** ChatGPT.
3. **Unsure?** Both have free tiers — trial each in your real workflow.

Confirm current pricing on each official site before subscribing.
