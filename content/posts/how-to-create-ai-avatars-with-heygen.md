HeyGen turns a script into a talking-head avatar video — no camera, studio or on-screen presenter required. Here's how to make one.

## Step-by-step

1. **Sign up** for HeyGen.
2. **Choose an avatar** — a stock avatar, or create a **custom one** (yours or a consenting person's).
3. **Enter your script** — type or paste the text the avatar will speak.
4. **Pick a voice and language** — match the tone and localize if needed.
5. **Generate** — HeyGen produces the talking-head video.
6. **Review and refine** — adjust script, pacing or voice for a natural result.

## Tips for natural results

- Write **conversationally** — formal scripts sound stilted
- Keep sentences **short** and easy to speak
- Add **natural pauses** where a person would breathe
- Test a **short clip first** before generating long videos

## Great use cases

- **Training and explainer** videos
- **Marketing and product** clips
- **Localized** versions in multiple languages
- **Faceless** channels and consistent on-brand presenters

Only create custom avatars of **yourself or people who have explicitly agreed**. Generating a video of someone's likeness without permission isn't just against the rules — it can be illegal and cause real harm. HeyGen builds in consent checks for exactly this reason. Used ethically, AI avatars are a genuine production shortcut; used carelessly, they're a liability.

## Bottom line

Create a HeyGen avatar video by choosing (or making) an avatar, entering a natural script, picking a voice and language, and generating. Keep scripts conversational and always respect consent. Confirm current features and pricing on HeyGen's site.
