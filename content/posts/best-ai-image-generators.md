There is no single best AI image generator — there's a best one *for your goal*. Want gallery-quality art? Commercial-safe assets? Free and offline? Each points to a different tool. Here's how the top options compare in 2026.

## Quick picks

| Your goal | Best tool |
| --- | --- |
| Artistic, stylized images | Midjourney |
| Easiest to use + edit | ChatGPT (built-in generator) |
| Commercial / brand-safe | Adobe Firefly |
| Free, local, controllable | Stable Diffusion or Flux |
| Posters with readable text | Ideogram |

> Image tools change pricing and version numbers frequently. Confirm current plans on each official site before subscribing.

## The contenders

- **Midjourney** — the benchmark for aesthetics and style. Now has a web app and editing tools beyond its Discord roots.
- **ChatGPT image generation** — conversational, easy, strong at following detailed prompts and rendering text. Best for non-experts.
- **Google Imagen / Gemini** — high quality, strong realism, integrated into Google's ecosystem.
- **Adobe Firefly** — built for commercial safety and licensing peace of mind, integrated into Photoshop.
- **Stable Diffusion / Flux** — open models you can run locally with full control (ControlNet, fine-tunes, plugins).
- **Ideogram** — best-in-class at rendering text inside images.

## The one section most guides skip: commercial safety

If you're making images for a business, *licensing matters more than looks*. Adobe Firefly is explicitly positioned for commercial-safe generation, which is why design teams favor it even when another tool produces prettier images. Always read the license tier of any tool before using outputs commercially.

## How to choose

1. **Chasing visual quality?** Midjourney.
2. **Want the easiest experience?** ChatGPT's built-in generator.
3. **Using images for a brand or client?** Adobe Firefly.
4. **Want free, offline or full control?** Stable Diffusion or Flux.
5. **Designing posters with text?** Ideogram.

Most people are best served by one easy tool (ChatGPT) plus one specialist (Midjourney for art or Firefly for commercial work).
