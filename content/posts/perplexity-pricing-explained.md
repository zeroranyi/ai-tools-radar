Perplexity's pricing is refreshingly simple — there's a free plan and a Pro plan. The nuance is in what each unlocks. Here's the breakdown. *Confirm live numbers on Perplexity's official pricing page.*

## The plans at a glance

| Plan | Roughly | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | Cited answers, standard searches, everyday research |
| **Pro** | ~$20/mo | Stronger models, higher advanced-search limits, file uploads, image generation |

## What free actually covers

Unusually for a free tier, Perplexity free does the core job well: ask, get a synthesized answer **with sources**. For casual fact-checking and everyday questions, you may never need to pay.

## What Pro adds

- Access to **more capable models**
- **Higher limits** on advanced/Pro searches
- **File uploads** (query your PDFs and docs)
- Image generation
- Generally deeper research runs

## Is Pro worth it?

> **The honest threshold:** upgrade when you start doing research *for work or study on a regular basis*, or when you want to analyze your own documents. If you just ask the occasional question, the free plan is the smarter choice.

## Don't forget the API

If you're a developer, Perplexity's **API is billed separately** from Pro — usage-based, not a flat subscription. Don't confuse the two when budgeting.

## Bottom line

For most people the decision is **Free vs Pro**, and free is genuinely capable. Move to Pro when frequent or document-heavy research makes the limits bite. Check Perplexity's official pricing page for current figures.
