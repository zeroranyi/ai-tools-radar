Perplexity's pricing is refreshingly simple — a free plan, Pro, and a higher Max tier. The nuance is in what each unlocks and how the separate API is billed. Here's the breakdown. *Prices are as of 2026 — confirm on Perplexity's official pricing page.*

## The plans at a glance

| Plan | Price (as of 2026) | What you get |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | Cited answers, standard searches, everyday research |
| **Pro** | ~$20/mo (~$200/yr) | Stronger models, higher Pro-search limits, file uploads, image generation |
| **Max** | ~$200/mo | Highest limits, earliest features, power users |
| **API** | Usage-based | Separate, pay-per-use for developers |

## What free actually covers

Unusually for a free tier, Perplexity free does the core job well: ask, get a synthesized answer **with sources**. For casual fact-checking and everyday questions, you may never need to pay.

## What Pro adds

- Access to **more capable models**
- **Higher limits** on advanced/Pro searches
- **File uploads** (query your PDFs and docs)
- Image generation
- Generally deeper research runs

## Pro vs Max

Most people choosing to pay want **Pro** (~$20). **Max** (~$200) is for heavy power users who want the highest limits and earliest features — a 10x jump that only makes sense at serious daily volume. Wondering whether Pro is worth it for you specifically? See [Is Perplexity Pro Worth It?](/posts/is-perplexity-pro-worth-it) for the decision breakdown.

## Don't forget the API

If you're a developer, Perplexity's **API is billed separately** from Pro — usage-based (per request/token), not a flat subscription. Don't confuse the two when budgeting: a Pro subscription doesn't include API credits.

## Bottom line

As of 2026: Free ($0), Pro (~$20/mo or ~$200/yr), Max (~$200/mo), plus a separate usage-based API. Free is genuinely capable; most paying users want Pro. Check Perplexity's official pricing page for current figures.
