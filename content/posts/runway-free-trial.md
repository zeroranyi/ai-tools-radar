Runway is a leading AI video tool, and its free plan lets you try before you buy. Here's what the free tier actually includes — and when you'll need to upgrade.

## What the free plan gives you

- **Limited credits** to generate AI video and use editing tools
- Access to **core features** so you can evaluate the tool
- Enough for **a few short clips** and experiments

## The limits to expect

- **Credits run out fast** — video generation is credit-hungry
- **Watermarks** on free exports
- **Resolution/length caps** on the free tier
- Best treated as an **evaluation**, not an ongoing free tool

## Free vs paid at a glance

| | Free | Paid |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Credits | Limited | Much more |
| Watermark | Usually yes | Removed |
| Resolution/length | Capped | Higher |
| Best for | Testing | Real projects |

> **Set your expectations right:** AI video burns credits far faster than text or images, so a free plan that sounds generous can evaporate in a handful of generations. Use the free tier for exactly what it's good at — *deciding whether Runway fits your workflow* — not as a free production tool. If video is core to your work, budget for a paid plan from the start; if it's occasional, the free credits plus careful use may stretch.

## Bottom line

Runway's free plan is a **try-before-you-buy** with limited credits, watermarks and caps — great for evaluating, not for ongoing production. Confirm current free-plan details and pricing on Runway's official site.
