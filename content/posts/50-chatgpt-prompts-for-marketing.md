The difference between useless and excellent ChatGPT output is the prompt. Each prompt below includes **[bracketed variables]** — replace them with your product, audience and goal. Skim to your channel and start customizing.

## How to use these prompts

Swap every `[bracket]` for your details. For best results, add your audience and desired tone. When format matters, ask for a table or numbered list. And remember: the output is a draft to refine, not a final to publish.

## SEO & content

1. `Act as an SEO strategist. Generate 10 blog title ideas targeting the keyword "[keyword]" for [audience], each under 60 characters.`
2. `Create an SEO content brief for the keyword "[keyword]": suggested H2s, questions to answer, and entities to mention.`
3. `Write an answer-first intro (40-60 words) that directly answers "[question]" for a blog post.`
4. `Generate 8 FAQ questions and concise answers about [topic] suitable for FAQ schema.`
5. `Cluster this keyword list into topic groups and label each by search intent: [paste keywords].`
6. `Suggest 10 internal linking opportunities for an article about [topic].`
7. `Rewrite this meta description to be under 155 characters and include the keyword "[keyword]": [paste].`

## Social media

8. `Write 5 LinkedIn posts about [topic] for [audience], each with a hook, 3 value points and a CTA. Tone: [tone].`
9. `Create 10 short tweets about [topic], each under 280 characters, mixing tips and questions.`
10. `Write 3 Instagram caption options for a post about [product], each with relevant hashtags.`
11. `Generate a 30-second TikTok script with a hook and outline promoting [product] to [audience].`
12. `Turn this blog post into 5 social posts for [platform]: [paste or summarize].`
13. `Write a week of daily social posts for [brand] around the theme [theme].`
14. `Suggest 10 hook lines that would stop the scroll for content about [topic].`

## Email marketing

15. `Write a 3-email welcome sequence for new subscribers of [product], aimed at [goal]. Include subject lines.`
16. `Write a re-engagement email for subscribers who haven't opened in 90 days. Tone: [tone].`
17. `Draft a product launch email for [product] highlighting [top 3 benefits].`
18. `Generate 8 subject line options for an email about [offer], optimized for open rate.`
19. `Write an abandoned-cart email for [product] with a friendly nudge and one incentive.`
20. `Turn these bullet points into a clear, scannable newsletter section: [paste].`

## Paid ads

21. `Generate 5 Google Ads headlines (≤30 chars) and 3 descriptions (≤90 chars) for [product] targeting [audience].`
22. `Write 5 Facebook ad variations for [product] using different angles: pain point, benefit, social proof, urgency, curiosity.`
23. `Create 3 ad hooks for [product] aimed at [audience], each testing a different emotion.`
24. `Write retargeting ad copy for people who visited [page] but didn't convert.`
25. `Suggest 10 audience targeting angles for advertising [product].`

## Copywriting & conversion

26. `Rewrite this product description using the PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution) framework: [paste].`
27. `Write a landing page for [offer]: headline, 3 benefit bullets, social proof line and CTA.`
28. `Generate 5 headline options for [page] using the "before/after/bridge" formula.`
29. `Rewrite this copy to be more concise and benefit-driven: [paste].`
30. `Write 3 CTA button variations for [action] and explain which is strongest.`
31. `Turn these features into customer benefits: [paste features].`

## Content strategy & research

32. `Create a 4-week content calendar for [brand] on [platform], with themes, post types and cadence.`
33. `Summarize the top pain points and objections of [target customer] buying [product category].`
34. `Suggest 10 content ideas for each stage of the funnel (awareness, consideration, decision) for [product].`
35. `Analyze this competitor's messaging and suggest 3 ways we can differentiate: [paste].`
36. `Brainstorm 15 blog topics that would attract [audience] to [product].`

## Video & short-form

37. `Write a YouTube video script outline about [topic] with a hook, 3 sections and an outro CTA.`
38. `Generate 10 short-form video ideas for [brand] in the [niche] space.`
39. `Write a 60-second explainer script for [product] in plain language.`

## Brand & messaging

40. `Create a brand voice guide for [brand]: tone, do's and don'ts, and 3 sample sentences.`
41. `Write a one-sentence positioning statement for [product] aimed at [audience].`
42. `Generate 5 taglines for [brand], ranging from playful to professional.`

## Analytics & optimization

43. `Suggest 5 A/B test ideas for [page or campaign] and what each would tell us.`
44. `Explain what these campaign results might mean and what to try next: [paste numbers].`
45. `Write a short report summarizing this month's marketing performance from these notes: [paste].`

## Customer & community

46. `Write 5 friendly responses to common customer questions about [product].`
47. `Draft a survey with 8 questions to learn why customers chose [product].`
48. `Write a thank-you message for new customers of [product] that encourages a review.`
49. `Turn this customer review into a short testimonial graphic caption: [paste].`
50. `Generate 10 community discussion prompts to boost engagement for [brand].`

> Pro tip: paste 2-3 samples of your existing content and ask ChatGPT to write "in this voice." Examples beat adjectives every time.
